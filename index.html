Obscura uses AES-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard in Galois/Counter Mode), a military-grade encryption standard widely adopted for securing sensitive data. Without the correct passphrase, it is virtually impossible to decrypt the message. AES-256 combined with a secure key derivation function like PBKDF2 and a unique salt (IV) ensures strong protection against brute-force attacks.
